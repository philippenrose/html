Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator(0 Deg. Latitude). Latitude is one of the main factors affecting temperature.This has to do with the shape of the earth.Another reason is due to the angle of solar incidence, this is the angle at which the Sun's rays strike the Earth's surface. At the Equator, the Sun's rays strike the Earth at a right angle, which makes the heat more intense and concentrated over a small area.
